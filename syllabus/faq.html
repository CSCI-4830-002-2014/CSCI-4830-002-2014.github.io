--- layout: syllabus title: FAQ group: syllabus --- How can I ask questions about the course?

If you have a question about a particular learning challenge topic, project milestone, or hackathon, use the Diqus forum on the course website. Each topic page should have its own dedicated Disqus forum. Your question can then get answered by other students and the teaching staff. If you have a question you wish to discuss in private, email the teaching staff.

How can I get extra help?

We strive to foster a highly collaborative learning environment in which you will have plenty of opporunities to help and get help from each other. If for some reason the learning environment is not sufficient and you need extra help, contact he teaching staff. We will arrange special office hours on a case-by-case basis.
